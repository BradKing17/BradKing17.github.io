Kojima Wars

A RTS/FPS game created in a cohort of 40 or so students, made as a spiritual successor to the Sega Genesis classic, Herzog Zwei.

The Game

Taking inspiration from the SEGA Genesis game, Herzog Zwei, Kojima Wars is a multiplayer FPS/RTS hybrid made as part of our cohorts commercial game dev module. This was our final game for the module and tasked our entire class with working in a studio-type setting, this project brought me a lot closer to people I previously had not worked with and gave me a massive appreciation for communication between teams and the importance of adequate planning before a project begins. This trailer was submitted as part of our group submission.
